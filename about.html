About Hronikka AI

What is Hronikka AI?

Hronikka AI is an artificial intelligence (AI) chatbot created to help users improve their diet and track their nutrition goals with human conversational dialogue. Using a message input prompt, users can ask AI questions and give information about themselves, and AI will respond with nutrition advice about how the user can improve their diet, which can include various food recipes and diet suggestions.

All information that the user enters will be safely stored, and based on the user's information, AI will track user progress on how close he is to achieving his goals for each day when the user is using thThose scores will be visible on the heat map chart, which will display different-colored dots for each day over the last 365 days. The dots will be colored differently based on the user's achieved score. A heat map will also allow user to see their previous interactions with AI.e app.

How does it work?

Hronikka AI was created in August of 2025 with the idea of helping users with their dietary habits using the Gemini Large language model. The Gemini large language model is extended with additional tools to help get more accurate data and responses. In the future, users will be allowed to pick the large language model they want to use for generating their responses.

What type of questions can you ask Hronikka AI?

Hronikka AI accepts a lot of different questions about nutrition. Users can ask different questions, like:

  • How to lose 5-7kg in the next 3 months?
  • How to improve my muscle tone?
  • What veggie meals have high protein and iron?
  • How to prep food for the week?

Hronikka AI can remember various questions to continue the conversation in a more fluid manner.

What are the limitations of Hronikka AI?

Even if it is a great tool, you still can encounter certain issues while using it:

  • It does not fully understand the complexity of human language. Hronikka AI is trained to generate words based on input. Because of this, responses might seem shallow and lack true insight.
  • Responses can sound like a machine and unnatural. Since Food Nutrition AI predicts the next word, it can overuse words such as the or and.
  • It summarizes but does not cite sources. Hronikka AI does not provide analysis or insight into any data or statistics, nor does it provide links to sources unless you specifically request them. Hronikka AI might provide statistics, but no real commentary on what these statistics mean or how they relate to the topic.
  • It cannot understand sarcasm and irony. Hronikka AI is based on a data set of text.
  • It might focus on the wrong part of a question and not be able to shift.
